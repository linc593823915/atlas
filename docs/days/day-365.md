## Focus

- Read the full Atlas architecture, the year-end retrospective, the final benchmark package, and the quarter review notes.

## Write

- Write the architecture defense summary, top five lessons, top five remaining weaknesses, and the next 6-12 month roadmap.

## Build

- Package the final graduation bundle, final documentation set, and last memory entry for the program.

## Output

- Final architecture defense note, specialization roadmap, graduation package, and annual memory entry.

## Daily Assessment Standard

- Score out of 20.
- Defense clarity and evidence quality: 5 points.
- Written retrospective quality: 5 points.
- Final documentation completeness: 5 points.
- Next-stage roadmap realism: 5 points.
- Passing bar: 16+ and the graduation package is complete.
- Strong bar: 19+ with a defensible specialization roadmap and reusable learning artifacts.

## Interview Questions

1. What architectural decision in Atlas most clearly shows growth from engineer to architect, and why?
2. If this system had to be handed to another team tomorrow, what evidence proves it is operable?
3. What are the two highest-leverage skills you still need to reach staff-level agent architecture capability?
