## Build

## Output

- A compilable or runnable core implementation with brief technical notes.

## Daily Assessment Standard

- Score out of 20.
- Read evidence: 5 points.
- Write artifact quality: 5 points.
- Build or lab progress: 5 points.
- Output clarity and evidence capture: 5 points.
- Passing bar: 12+ and the planned artifact exists.
- Strong bar: 16+ with explicit trade-offs and one improvement idea beyond the task.

## Interview Questions

1. Walk through the control flow or data flow of the core path you are building.
2. What invariant must always hold if this implementation is correct?
3. Which abstraction are you choosing to protect future change, and why?
