# Review Calendar

Last updated: 2026-06-29

## Purpose

This calendar converts the roadmap into a predictable review rhythm.

## Weekly Rhythm

- Day 1: scope and reading check
- Day 2: RFC or interface checkpoint
- Day 5: validation checkpoint
- Day 6: documentation and review checkpoint
- Day 7: retrospective and memory checkpoint

## Monthly Rhythm

- Week 1 start: monthly objective and issue-board confirmation
- Week 2 middle: progress pulse check
- Week 4 end: mentor review and mock interview
- Month close: OKR grading, backlog rollover, and evidence pack update

## Quarterly Rhythm

- final week of the quarter: architecture walkthrough
- final week of the quarter: mock panel interview
- final week of the quarter: quarter replay pack completion
- quarter close: promotion-signal calibration and next-quarter planning

## Year-End Rhythm

- final benchmark review
- graduation architecture defense
- next-stage specialization planning

## Scheduling Rule

- Every review must point to a specific evidence pack.
- Every major Atlas milestone must have both a review date and a replay date.
