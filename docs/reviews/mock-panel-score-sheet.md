# Mock Panel Score Sheet

Last updated: 2026-06-29

## Purpose

Use this score sheet for monthly and quarterly mock interview panels.

The panel evaluates the learner as a future AI agent architect, using Atlas as the evidence base.

## Panel Composition

- 1 coding interviewer
- 1 system design interviewer
- 1 reliability and operations interviewer
- 1 behavioral and leadership interviewer
- optional 1 documentation and review interviewer

## Scoring Scale

- `1`
  weak signal, major gaps, cannot defend reasoning
- `2`
  mixed signal, partial understanding, needs heavy support
- `3`
  solid signal, good reasoning, acceptable quality
- `4`
  strong signal, architect-level clarity, teaches while answering
