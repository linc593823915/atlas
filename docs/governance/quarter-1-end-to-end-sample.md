# Quarter 1 End-to-End Sample

Last updated: 2026-06-29

## Purpose

This sample shows a full Quarter 1 chain from monthly scorecards to replay, KPI review, steering sample, and executive summary.

## Scope

- month 01 to month 03 scorecards
- quarter 1 KPI sheet
- quarter 1 replay pack
- quarterly steering review sample
- executive dashboard sample

## Chain

1. Monthly scorecards provide the quarter’s recurring evidence base.
2. The quarter KPI sheet compresses quarter-level metrics.
3. The replay pack summarizes quarter evidence and trend questions.
4. The steering review sample shows how a committee would discuss the quarter.
5. The executive dashboard sample shows the sponsor-facing readout.
