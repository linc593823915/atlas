# Promotion Packet Sample

Last updated: 2026-06-29

## Purpose

This sample shows what a promotion-readiness packet summary could look like.

## Packet Summary

- packet date: 2026-12-31
- target level: strong L5-like
- readiness conclusion: `nearly ready`

## Strongest Evidence

- Atlas evolved from a shell service into a multi-layer agent platform with config, tools, MCP, orchestration, evals, governance, and operations.
- Monthly and quarterly artifacts were consistently produced, reviewed, and linked to engineering work.
- System design reasoning improved from local module concerns to platform-level trade-offs.

## Weakest Evidence

- production benchmark evidence is still narrower than ideal
- some governance and operational paths are documented more strongly than they are practiced under live load

## Evidence Cited

- monthly scorecards
- quarterly KPI sheets
- quarter replay packs
- Atlas backlog completion trail
- representative issue cards with validation evidence

## Committee Summary

- strongest signal:
  sustained execution plus increasing architectural judgment
- weakest signal:
  limited real-scale production evidence
- decision:
  promote when one more quarter of production-style reliability evidence is added
- next review date:
  next quarterly steering review
