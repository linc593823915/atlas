# Month 01 End-to-End Sample

Last updated: 2026-06-29

## Purpose

This sample shows a complete Month 01 chain from issue execution to scorecard and review.

## Scope

- week 01 to week 04 reports
- month 01 scorecard
- month 01 review packet
- month 01 issue snapshot
- representative issue cards
- representative evidence references

## Chain

1. Weekly reports define the weekly execution trail.
2. Issue cards define the concrete Atlas work units.
3. The issue snapshot summarizes board state at month close.
4. The scorecard summarizes monthly metrics and judgment.
5. The review packet records the mentor and promotion signal.
