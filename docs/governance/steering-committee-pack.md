# Steering Committee Pack

Last updated: 2026-06-29

## Purpose

This pack defines what a steering committee should review when overseeing the one-year AI agent architect program.

## Committee Goals

- confirm the program is producing real engineering growth
- confirm Atlas is improving as a system, not just as documentation
- confirm the learner is on track for the target role
- decide where to accelerate, de-scope, or intervene

## Required Inputs

- Program Executive Summary
- Annual Readiness Matrix
- Quarterly KPI Sheet
- Quarter Replay Pack
- latest Monthly Scorecards
- current Atlas backlog and issue-board snapshot

## Committee Questions

1. Is the roadmap still aligned with the target role?
2. Is Atlas improving in the right dimensions?
3. What evidence most strongly supports readiness claims?
4. What evidence most strongly challenges readiness claims?
5. What should change in the next quarter?

## Decision Options

- continue as planned
- increase rigor
- reduce scope and improve quality
- add mentor support
- add review frequency
- re-sequence the roadmap

## Output

- steering decision
- key risks
- required corrections
- quarter focus update
